Dr. Sushil Singla

Director - Paediatrics

Information

Dr. Sushil Singla is a renowned Paediatrician based at Sarvodaya Hospital & Research Centre having a vast experience of 20 years in the field of Paediatrics & Neonatology. He has been heading the Department of Paediatrics & Neonatology and his determination has helped Sarvodaya in developing one of the most advanced Neonatal Units in Delhi NCR.
