Privacy Policy

At Care Point Hospital, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact number, email, age, gender, address, etc.
  • Medical Details: Health records, symptoms, prescriptions, appointment history.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, interaction data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us to:

  • Provide accurate medical consultation and follow-up.
  • Book appointments and send reminders.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send health updates, newsletters, or promotional messages (only with your consent).

3. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to collect analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

4. Data Security

We follow strict security protocols to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Data is encrypted and stored securely.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Data may be shared with trusted healthcare providers or legal authorities when required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to request corrections
  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined or as required by applicable laws.

8. Updates to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
