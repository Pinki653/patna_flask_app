About Us

Bringing Healthcare and Compassion Together

Healing Touch Hospital

Healing Touch Hospital is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility offering a wide range of medical services, from emergency care to specialized treatments. Our dedicated team ensures the best outcomes for our patients.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality, and affordable healthcare services to our community.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with dignity and respect.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in medical care.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and treatments.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty.
  • Community: We are committed to improving public health.

Care Point Hospital

Welcome to Care Point Hospital, where compassionate care meets modern medicine. Established in 2012, Care Point is a 300+ bed multi-specialty healthcare institution offering advanced treatments and a people-first philosophy.

Our Journey

From a humble 20-bed facility to a regional leader in healthcare, Care Point Hospital has grown by putting people over profits and excellence over convenience.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and accessible healthcare provider by delivering holistic, innovative, and patient-centered care.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • High-quality and evidence-based treatment
  • Affordable healthcare for every segment of society
  • Patient-first environment and infrastructure
  • Community awareness programs and outreach
  • Continuous innovation in research and care

Facilities

  • 24x7 Emergency Services
  • Advanced Diagnostic Labs & Modern OTs
  • Fully-equipped ICU and NICU
  • Blood Bank and Pharmacy
  • Telemedicine & Video Consultation

Why Choose Care Point?

  • Skilled and experienced professionals
  • Transparent billing systems
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Safe, clean, and healing environment

Community Outreach

We host awareness drives, vaccination camps, blood donation events, and rural health programs to serve beyond our walls.
