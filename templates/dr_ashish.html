Dr. Ashish Tomar

Senior Consultant - Orthopaedics & Spine Surgery

Department: Orthopaedics, Spine Surgery

Information

Dr. Ashish Tomar is a highly skilled and accomplished spine surgeon specializing in Minimally Invasive Spine Surgeries & Deformity Correction of the Spine. With a profound passion for restoring patients' quality of life, Dr. Tomar has dedicated his career to the advancement of minimally invasive techniques, revolutionizing the field of spinal surgery. He has successfully treated numerous patients suffering from spinal disorders. Driven by a patient-centric approach, he combines cutting-edge technology with his surgical proficiency to minimize discomfort, accelerate recovery, and optimize outcomes. He has performed North India's First OLIF L5-S1 and Faridabad's First OLIF, ALIF, and Scoliosis Surgery.

Speciality

  • Scoliosis & Deformity of Spine
  • Prolapsed Disc
  • Tumours of Spine
  • Spinal Stenosis
  • Fracture Spine
  • Infections and TB Spine
  • Failed Back Syndrome
  • Revision Spine Surgery
  • Acetabulum, Pelvis, and other Fractures and Trauma
