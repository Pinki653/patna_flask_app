Dr. Sujoy Bhattacharjee

HOD & Director - Robotic Joint Replacement

Department: Orthopaedics, Robotic Joint Replacement

Information

Dr. Sujoy Bhattacharjee is renowned for being the best robotic knee replacement surgeon who has been performing Joint Replacement Surgeries for 20 years with experience of 25,000+ Joint Replacements & 5,000+ Cruciate Retaining Robotic Joint Replacement Surgeries. He leads the Robotic Joint Replacement Program at Sarvodaya Hospital, which performed the World’s First Cruciate Retaining Robotic Joint Replacement.

  • Partial Knee Replacement
  • Revision Joints Replacement Surgeries
  • Shoulder and Elbow Replacement
  • Sports Injury (Arthroscopy)
