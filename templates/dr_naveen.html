Dr. Naveen Sanchety

Director - Surgical Oncology

Department: Surgical Oncology, Cancer Care

Information

Dr. Naveen Sanchety is a highly skilled oncosurgeon specialising in breast, head and neck, gynaecological, gastrointestinal, and sarcoma cancers. With DNB qualifications in Oncosurgery and Surgery, he is also a certified robotic surgeon and holds fellowships from prestigious surgical associations. Dr. Sanchety's career spans over a decade, with senior roles in multiple renowned hospitals across India. He is a dedicated researcher with numerous publications and presentations, particularly in breast cancer. Recognised for his academic excellence, he received the best paper award at NATCON 2011. He serves actively in several oncology organisations and is a founding member of the Young Oncologist Group of India. He also contributes to the Asian Journal of Oncology as an editorial board member.
