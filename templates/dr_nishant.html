Dr. Nishant Soni

Consultant - Orthopaedics & Hand Surgery

Department: Orthopaedics

Information

Dr. Nishant Soni is an experienced Orthopaedic and Micro Surgeon based in Delhi, specializing in hand, wrist, and microvascular surgeries. His expertise spans across limb reconstruction, peripheral nerve surgery, paediatric orthopaedics, and complex soft tissue reconstructions.

Speciality

  • Hand, Wrist and Microvascular Surgery
  • Soft Tissue Reconstruction
  • Brachial Plexus Reconstruction
  • Peripheral Nerve Surgery
  • Congenital Hand Anomalies & Paediatric Ortho
  • Upper Limb Trauma & Replacement Arthroplasties
  • Bone and Soft Tissue Malignancies and Reconstruction

Education

  • AISSE & AISSCE - Fr. Agnel School, Delhi
  • MBBS - Jammu University (1997-2003)
  • MS (Ortho) - RMMCH, Annamalai University (2006-2009)
  • Fellowship - Hand & Microvascular Surgery, KMC Manipal (2013-2014)
  • Microsurgery Training - Ganga Hospital, Coimbatore (2014)
  • Visiting Scholar - Chang Gung Memorial Hospital, Taiwan
  • Diploma - Limb Reconstruction & Microsurgery, Barcelona, Spain (2016-17)
  • Fellowship - Toolo Hospital, Helsinki, Finland

Experience

  • Senior Resident, Lal Bahadur Hospital, Delhi (2009–2011)
  • Senior Resident, Guru Teg Bahadur Hospital, Delhi (2011–2013)
  • Faculty & Fellow, KMC Manipal (2013–2014)
  • Assistant Professor, CMC Ludhiana (2014)
  • Consultant, QRG Central Hospital, Faridabad (2014–2015)
  • Consultant, IBS Hospital, Delhi (2015–2016)
  • Visiting Scholar - Reconstructive Microsurgery, Taiwan (2016)
  • Senior Consultant - Medanta the Medicity (2017–2019)
  • Senior Consultant - Max Hospital, Saket & Gurugram (2019–2021)
