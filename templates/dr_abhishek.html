Dr. Abhishek Raj

Senior Consultant & Head (Unit I) - Medical Oncology, Haematology & BMT

Departments: Medical Oncology, Haematology & BMT, Cancer Care, Genomic Oncology, Paediatric Oncology

Information

Dr. Abhishek Raj is the Senior Consultant & Head (Unit I) of Medical Oncology, Haematology & BMT at Sarvodaya Hospital, with vast experience in cancer treatment and bone marrow transplantation. He holds a DM in Medical Oncology from AIIMS, New Delhi, after completing his MD in Internal Medicine from the University College of Medical Sciences and MBBS from Maulana Azad Medical College, Delhi.

He specializes in chemotherapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and bone marrow transplantation. His expertise spans solid tumors such as lung, breast, gastrointestinal, and urologic cancers, as well as acute and chronic leukemia. He has held key positions at BLK-Max Hospital, Aakash Healthcare, and Fortis Memorial Research Institute.
