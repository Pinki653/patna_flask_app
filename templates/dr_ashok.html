Dr. (Lt Col) Ashok Kumar

Senior Consultant & Head - Radiation Oncology

Department: Radiation Oncology, Cancer Care

Information

Dr. (Lt. Col) Ashok Kumar is a distinguished Radiation Oncologist with more than 23 years of experience in treating cancers such as head and neck, brain, gynecologic, and genitourinary malignancies. He is a pioneer in Brachytherapy, with notable contributions in clinical practice, research, and teaching. He is dedicated to comprehensive cancer care and has received several fellowships and honors throughout his career.
